##  **The Waterloo "Red Zone"**

Waterloo is the epicenter of this crisis due to high-stakes academic competition and a high "international/out-of-province" student ratio.

### **The ROI Calculation**

The economic value of Wanders is measured by the prevention of "Academic Flight":

Value = (R(student) * C(tution)) + (H(saving) * P(reduction))

Where R is retention, C is tution, H is healthcare cost, and P is visit reduction.

## **✅ The Wanders Solution: Peer-to-Peer Integration**

Wanders targets the **16-24 demographic** by replacing "Clinical Waitlists" with "Community Action."

1. **Micro-Interests:** Connecting via "Badminton at 3 PM" instead of "Networking."  
2. **Upstream Intervention:** Catching the **50% of students** in distress before they require emergency care.  
3. **1.64x Improvement:** Students engaging in regular group activities are 64% more likely to report "Positive Mental Health."
